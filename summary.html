Methodology

Aggregates are computed from the IPEA detail extracts loaded in data/expenses.json. Negative amounts (adjustments and repayments) are included in totals as published by IPEA. "Members" counts unique parliamentarians who appeared in any loaded reporting period. Salary estimates are derived from Remuneration Tribunal determinations and are not yet integrated into this build.

Source: IPEA.
