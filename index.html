About this site

This site transcribes published expense reports for Australian Federal parliamentarians (Members of the House of Representatives and Senators) into searchable form. Source data is from the Independent Parliamentary Expenses Authority via data.gov.au.

Errors may have been introduced in transcription. Verify figures against the original reports where it matters. To rebuild with up-to-date or historical data, fork the repository and run python scripts/fetch_data.py.

Built on Independent Parliamentary Expenses Authority data published under CC BY 3.0 AU. Salary estimates derived from Remuneration Tribunal determinations. Interest data sourced from the Register of Members' Interests and Register of Senators' Interests.

This site is not affiliated with the Commonwealth of Australia, IPEA, or any parliamentarian.
